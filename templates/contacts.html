🔴 High Risk Alert
Email sent immediately when cardiac risk is High (>66%). Recommended for all contacts.
🟡 Medium Risk Alert
Advisory email when risk is Moderate (33–66%). Optional — recommended for doctors and primary caregivers.
Tip: Enable both High & Medium for doctors. Family members usually only need the High Risk alert.

How Alerts Work

🔴 High Risk: Immediate email is sent to contacts who have High Risk Alert enabled. Includes patient vitals and an urgent action recommendation.

🟡 Medium Risk: Advisory email is sent to contacts who have Medium Risk Alert enabled. Recommends a medical consultation within a few days.

🟢 Low Risk: No alert is sent to any contact. Everything looks normal.

Your privacy is protected — you will never see your own risk level. Only your trusted emergency contacts receive risk information.
