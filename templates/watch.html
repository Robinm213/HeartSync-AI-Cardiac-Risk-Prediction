How to Connect Your Noise IND0925 to HeartSync

Your Noise watch doesn't have a direct API. Use this bridge method (Watch → NoiseFit → Google Fit → HeartSync):

1
Install the NoiseFit app

Download NoiseFit from Google Play Store and pair your IND0925 watch via Bluetooth.

2
Enable Google Fit sync

In NoiseFit → Profile → Connected Apps → enable Google Fit. Your watch will now push heart rate, SpO₂, steps, sleep and stress data to Google Fit automatically.

3
Connect HeartSync to Google Fit

On this page, tap "Connect Google Fit" below and sign in with the same Google account NoiseFit is syncing to. HeartSync will then read your live watch data.

4
Use it on the Predict page

Once connected, go to Predict → "From Watch". HeartSync will auto-fill Heart Rate, SpO₂, Sleep, Stress and Activity Minutes directly from your watch. You only need to fill the remaining health fields manually.

5
Alternative: CSV export

If Google Fit isn't an option: NoiseFit → Profile → Health Data → Export → download the CSV → upload it on this Watch page using the CSV upload card below.

* Blood pressure, cholesterol and blood glucose must be entered manually — your watch does not measure these.

How to Connect Your Samsung Galaxy Fit 3 / Watch to HeartSync

Samsung uses Health Connect as the bridge between Samsung Health and Google Fit. The path is: Galaxy Fit 3 → Samsung Health → Health Connect → Google Fit → HeartSync.

1
Pair Galaxy Fit 3 with Samsung Health

Install Samsung Health from Play Store and pair your Galaxy Fit 3 (or Galaxy Watch) over Bluetooth. The Galaxy Wearable app may also prompt — install it if asked.

2
Install / open Health Connect

Most newer Samsung phones already have Health Connect installed. If not, install it from Play Store. (Settings → Apps → search "Health Connect".)

3
Connect Samsung Health → Health Connect

Open Samsung Health → tap your profile (top-right) → Settings → Health Connect → tap "Connect to Health Connect" → allow all permissions (heart rate, steps, sleep, oxygen, calories).

4
Connect Google Fit → Health Connect

Install Google Fit from Play Store and sign in with your Gmail. Open Fit → Profile → Settings → Manage connected apps → tap Health Connect → enable. Grant all data access checkboxes.

5
Wear the watch for 5 minutes

Heart rate must be measured at least once for the data pipeline to fill. Then open the Google Fit app and pull-to-refresh — you should see today's heart rate, steps, sleep, etc.

6
Connect HeartSync

Below on this page, click "Connect Google Fit" and sign in with the same Gmail you used in Google Fit + as a Test User in Google Cloud. HeartSync will now read your Galaxy Fit 3 data automatically.

7
Alternative: CSV export

If Health Connect isn't an option: Samsung Health → Profile → Settings → Download personal data → choose date range → CSV → upload via the CSV card below.

❤️ Heart Rate 🩸 SpO₂ 🚶 Steps 😴 Sleep Hours 🧠 Stress Level 🔥 Calories Burned
